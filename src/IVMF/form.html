Welcome and Instructions

Thank you for your interest in V-WISE! Before you begin your application, please review the following guidelines:

  • The V-WISE program is able to accept applications from all active women service members, all honorably discharged women service members, and women spouses/life partners of all honorably discharged service members or active duty service members.

  • if you have not already visited our FAQ page, we encourage you to do so now to familiarize yourself with the out of pocket costs and frequently asked questions.
  • As part of your application, we will be requesting you or your spouse/life partner’s military information (including your/his/her DD 214 or NG 22 indicating honorable discharge, or your/his/her DD 220 or most recent LES indicating active service). We urge you to gather this information before you start, however, you may save and return to complete the application at any time.

    Note: If you are applying as a military spouse/life partner and do not share a last name with your veteran/service member, you will need to submit documentation of the marriage or life partner relationship. Acceptable documents include any one of the following: marriage license; named parents in a co-parenting or adoption agreement; joint marriage, lease, or title; joint ownership of motor vehicle or bank account or credit card account; designation of domestic partner as a primary beneficiary of employee's life insurance, retirement benefits, or will; assignment of a durable Power of Attorney or Health Care Proxy to one another.
  • When you submit your application, you will receive an email from vwise@syr.edu confirming your application was submitted successfully. Please add this email address to your safe senders list. If you do not receive a confirmation email after completing and submitting your application that means it was not received by our system. Please visit our troubleshooting tips page to resubmit your application or request help.

If your application is accepted and you participate in a V-WISE program, you have the choice to allow V-WISE to share your contact information (Name, Address, Phone Number, Email, Business Name, Industry, and Website) with the U.S. Small Business Administration’s Office of Veterans Business Development (SBA OVBD). When this info is shared with SBA OVBD during Phase I of the program, SBA OVBD will review your business information to connect you with a business mentor within their network (SBA, SBDC, SCORE, VBOC, WBC, PTAC), https://www.sba.gov/tools/local-assistance, to support you as you start or grow your business.

Program Selection

All currently available V-WISE courses are full. If your application is approved, you will be added to our distribution list to receive an email notification as soon as the date/location for the next online course and conference are determined. You will need to respond to the email notification to enroll in the program. Please keep in mind that only 200 seats are available for each location - enrollment into each program is based on a first come, first served basis.

Once you have enrolled in an available date/location, a request to provide your registration fee and select your breakout session classes will be sent 3 - 4 months before the conference. Your seat in the class is not confirmed until your application is approved and you have completed the registration process.
